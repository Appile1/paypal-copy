Why PayPal

PayPal was created to provide a secure and convenient online payment solution, initially conceived as a way to transfer money digitally between Palm Pilots. Co-founders Max Levchin, Peter Thiel, and Luke Nosek aimed to address the challenges of traditional payment methods, fostering a simplified and accessible platform for e-commerce transactions.

Founderas of PayPal

Max Levchin

Max Levchin, born in Ukraine in 1975, is a computer scientist and entrepreneur. He co-founded PayPal in December 1998 with the initial goal of developing security software to combat fraud in online transactions.

Last updated : 3 mins ago
Peter Thiel

Peter Thiel, born in Germany in 1967, is a renowned entrepreneur, venture capitalist, and author. He co-founded PayPal with a vision to revolutionize online payments and later became one of the first outside investors in Facebook.
